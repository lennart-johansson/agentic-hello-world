Meet the Gang

Three legends of the African plains. Long necks, big personalities, zero patience for short trees.

Arlo

"The Gentle Giant"

Origin
Northern Serengeti, Tanzania — born at sunrise near the Grumeti River
Diet
Strictly acacia leaves, young shoots, and the occasional wild fig when no one's watching
Temperament
Patient and thoughtful. Arlo never rushes — except when there are figs involved
Appearance
Warm amber patches, a long dark mane, and the most expressive eyes on the savanna

Bruto

"The Bold Adventurer"

Origin
Masai Mara, Kenya — raised near the Mara River where he once stared down a hippo
Diet
Anything tall enough to be a challenge. Thorny branches are just a bonus crunch
Temperament
Bold, headstrong, and always first to check out anything new. Basically a 5-metre golden retriever
Appearance
Deep russet patches, a broad chest, and an impressive set of ossicones that he's very proud of

Spotty

"The Social Butterfly"

Origin
Okavango Delta, Botswana — the most photogenic corner of the continent
Diet
Acacia, baobab blossoms, and dew-kissed leaves collected before sunrise like a true connoisseur
Temperament
Charismatic and endlessly curious. Spotty knows every animal on the plains — and their gossip
Appearance
Perfectly symmetrical cream-and-chestnut spots, a graceful neck, and a swishy tail she uses to dramatic effect
